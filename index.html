Interesting facts about Origami

  1. Origami is a Japanese art.
  2. Origami is made up of 2 Japanese words, ori meaning folded and kami meaning paper.
  3. The most simple origami shape to make is the crane which only requires a few folds but turns out to look amazing.
  4. The UK has a national magazine dedicated to the art of Origami! The magazine started a origami making competion from 1970 and is still going on now!

Boat

  1. The origami boat is a reltivly easy shape to make and sometimes people use it to have paper boat races in some small ponds.
  2. Some people also make the boat out of newspaper for more durability.
  3. You can decorate the boat with paint and diffrent colors so the boat will look amzing while it is floating in the pond.

Frog

  1. The origami frog is a popular shape in origame because of it's bouncing feature.
  2. If you gently press on the back of the frog and let go like a spring the frog will bounce.
  3. The origami frog is also used in some board games in Japan and kids like to have competions with it.
